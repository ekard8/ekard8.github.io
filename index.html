My name is Drake Flood, and I'm currently a fourth-year architecture student at the University of Houston College of Architecture and Design.

As a designer and writer, I'm interested in the confluence of art, architecture, technology, and criticality. I believe architecture is best understood when it is seen through its multiplicitous nature. As designers, we have the primary responsibility to imagine brighter and better futures.
