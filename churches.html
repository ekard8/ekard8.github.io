Procedural Church Generator

Procedural Church Generator was an exploration into procedurally generated floor plans. The project looked at the classical, architectural concept of cross-in-plan churches. The Grasshopper definition attempts to replicate their form while simultaneously loosening and randomizing the geometric restrictions. Resulting in a definition, that is capable of creating an infinite number of unique churches each with its own complex and provocative spatial relationships.
