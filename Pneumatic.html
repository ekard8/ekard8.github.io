Pneumatic Analysis

This set of drawings came out of a research exploration into understanding pneumatics. The class started by reading the Inflatocookbook by Antfarm and was followed by an intense search for precedents. I was tasked with focusing on pneumatic structures used as art installations. These ranged from massive scale installations, like Leviathan by Anish Kapoor, to smaller scale experiments, like Silver Clouds by Andy Warhol. This was also my first foray into simulation. Many of these models were created by using Kangaroo to simulate their inflation. These 3D models could then be used to produce a suite of drawings and diagrams.
