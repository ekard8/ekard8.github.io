Pneumatic Analysis

This project analyzes downtown Houston with specialzied tools. Downtown Houston, in reality, is less of a conventional city and more of a board game where life unfolds. It’s a game of chutes and ladders that can haphazardly transport you to experiences outside of reality. A game that is played at multiple scales of movement, from the tunnels, to the asphalt, to the highway. It’s a game that is affected by forces ranging from the microscopic to the universal. And all of it driven by an engine of real estate and consumption propped up on the back of the petrochemical industry. To tell this story, the project tracks the views and experiences of an office worker who traverses through the exclusionary spaces of the city on a day-to-day basis.
